ISSUE: Hub "Cutting Corners"
-----------------------------
The central hub of the labyrinth is currently 13x13 tiles (`HUB_SIZE = 13` in `level1.ts`). 
The maze grid uses a cell step of 12 tiles (6 tiles floor + 6 tiles wall).
Because 13 is not a multiple of 12 (and not centered perfectly on a wall/cell boundary triplet), 
the hub carving loop partially overlaps the 6x6 wall joints at the corners of the hub.
This leaves L-shaped slivers of walls instead of clean boundaries.

OBJECTIVE:
----------
Modify `level1.ts` so the hub does not "cut corners" of the walls.

SUGGESTED FIX:
--------------
Change `HUB_SIZE` to a grid-aligned value:
- `HUB_SIZE = 18` (covers 1 cell + both adjacent walls: 6+6+6)
- `HUB_SIZE = 6` (covers exactly 1 cell)
- Any value that follows the formula `N * CELL_SIZE + (N+1) * WALL_SIZE` or similar grid-snapping.

FILES PROVIDED:
---------------
- level1.ts: Core maze generation and hub logic.
- index.ts: Shared constants (SPAWN_DISTANCE, CELL_SIZE, etc).
- physics.ts: Collision logic and solid tile definitions.
- Room.ts: Server-side room management (calls generateMaze).
- ARCHITECTURE.md: Project overview and rendering rules.
